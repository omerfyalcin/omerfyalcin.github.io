Welcome.

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Political Science and Social Data Analytics at the Pennsylvania State University and am advised by Bruce Desmarais. I have previously studied at Humboldt University in Berlin, Germany and at Bogazici University in Istanbul, Turkey.

I study (authoritarian) ruling coalitions using network theory, and their implications for long-term regime outcomes like personalization of dictatorships, accountability, governance, and policy. I am also interested in text data, specifically, Arabic text data for NLP tasks like entity-recognition. Relatedly, I have a regional interest in the Arab world.
