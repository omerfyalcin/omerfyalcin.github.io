Dissertation “Essays on Political Elite Networks”- (Working Title)
In this project, I broadly investigate how political elites form networks and the implications of these networks on the regime in question. In a theoretical paper, I show howindividual actors respond to ideological and financial incentives when seeking allies. I also contribute to the empirical study of elite networks by making use of an unconven-tional graph database, Wikidata, that allows for the measurement of multiple layersof networks, dynamically, and across countries.
